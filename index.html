Everything you need

One system for your entire plant

From the moment an order arrives to the moment parts ship — every step is tracked.

📋
Orders & scheduling

Receive customer orders, assign them to machines and arms, and track progress in real time against due dates.

🏭
Shop floor kiosk

Tablet-based kiosk with PIN authentication. Operators log cycles, scrap, QC, secondary ops, and maintenance without leaving the floor.

✅
Quality control

Inspection queues, configurable sampling bands, pass/rework/fail decisions, QC checklists per product, and full inspection history.

📦
Inventory

Real-time stock of raw materials, finished goods, WIP, and spare parts. Low-stock alerts and supplier purchasing dashboard.

🔧
Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance tasks per machine with overdue alerts, completion logging, and templates that never rewrite history.

📊
Reports & displays

Production, quality, and commercial reports. Configurable wall displays show live plant status on any TV or monitor.

💰
Job costing

Labour, material, and overhead tracked per order. Know your actual margin on every job — not just in hindsight.

🚢
Shipping & CoC

Dispatch finished goods, print packing slips, and generate Certificates of Conformance for food-grade and regulated products.

⚡
NCR & CAPA

Non-conformance reports with corrective and preventive action tracking. Quality incidents managed from report to resolution.
