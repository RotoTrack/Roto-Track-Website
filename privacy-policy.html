Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2026  ·  Pasya Group LLC

This Privacy Policy explains how Pasya Group LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the RotoTrack platform ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, company name, and other information you provide during registration.

Customer Data: Information you enter into the Service in the course of your business operations, including production data, inventory records, employee records, quality control data, customer records, and other manufacturing data ("Customer Data"). This data belongs to you.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you use the Service, including pages visited, features used, and actions taken. This helps us improve the Service.

Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, and similar technical information collected automatically when you access the Service.

Payment Information: Payment details are collected and processed by our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers on our systems.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (password resets, billing notifications, scheduled reports you configure)
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Improve and develop the Service
  • Monitor for security threats and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information or Customer Data to third parties. We do not use your Customer Data to train AI models or for any purpose other than providing the Service to you.

3. AI Features

RotoTrack includes optional AI-powered features (AI Help, AI Scrap Diagnosis, AI Shift Summary) powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you use these features, relevant data from your session is sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. This data is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. We do not use your data to train AI models. AI feature usage is tracked per company for usage limit purposes only.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party providers to operate the Service, including Supabase (database hosting), Vercel (application hosting), Resend (email delivery), Anthropic (AI features), and Stripe (payment processing). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to provide services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: If Pasya Group LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers provided by Supabase, located in the United States. We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage, and access controls.

No security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee that unauthorized parties will never be able to defeat our security measures. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and Customer Data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we will retain your data for thirty (30) days to allow you to export it, after which we will delete it from our active systems. Backups may persist for an additional period consistent with our backup retention schedule.

We may retain certain information longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update or correct your account information at any time through the Settings module.
  • Export: You may export your Customer Data at any time using the data export features in Settings.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@roto-track.com.
  • Email Preferences: You may configure which automated report emails you receive through Settings → Production → Report Emails.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Service uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and improve your experience. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, but this may affect the functionality of the Service.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a notice in the Service at least thirty (30) days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
